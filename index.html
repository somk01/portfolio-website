Hi, I'm Somya
Kateja.

I instinctively see stories in conversations, campaigns,
characters, and everything in between.

I'm currently pursuing my undergraduate studies at IIM Bangalore, where I'm increasingly drawn to the worlds of startups, marketing, consumer behaviour, and creative thinking.

What excites me most are ideas - the kind that make people pause, feel something, start a conversation, or see a brand differently. Whether it's a campaign concept, a story idea, or a small everyday observation, I'm fascinated by what makes certain ideas memorable enough to stay with us forever.

This website is where I explore those questions through ad scripts, campaign concepts, stories, and creative experiments.

01 — About

A little about me

I grew up in Udaipur, Rajasthan, and have always been curious about people, stories, and what turns an idea into something people genuinely care about.

Today, that curiosity shapes much of what I do.
At IIM Bangalore, I work with the Social Media Team, contributing to content ideation, creative strategy, and communication initiatives across the program.
I also serve as the Zonal Representative for the west zone, helping foster engagement within the student community.

Outside of that, I'm constantly exploring storytelling, advertising, and creative communication through personal projects and experiments.
From ad scripts and campaign concepts to story ideas and creative explorations, I'm fascinated by how narratives shape perception, spark emotion,
and help brands connect more meaningfully with their audiences.

03 — Work

My work

Most of the work you'll find here started with a simple question:

"What if there was a story here?"

What if a song knew you were moving on before you did?

What if a family conversation became a campaign?

What if an everyday observation could become a story?

This collection is simply my attempt to explore those questions through storytelling, advertising, and creative ideas.
