Kisi Ko Toh Pata Tha

A love story — told in episodes.

Anadya is back in town for her best friend Jiya's wedding. She's been away, focused on work, moving forward — or at least that's what she's been telling herself. But weddings have a way of bringing things back. And one of those things is Mahir — Jiya's brother, two years older, annoyingly present, and the one person Anadya had quietly decided she was completely over.

Episode 01

The Phone Call

Jiya— Kahaan hai tu? Koi itna late aata hai kya?
Anadya— Bas aa rahi hoon. Cab karwa rahi hoon.
Jiya— Jaldi kar…

A voice cuts in from Jiya's side.

Mahir(spotting Jiya on her phone)— Oye, chal na — kaun hai?
Jiya— Anadyaaa.
Anadya(softly)— Ye aa gaya?
Jiya— Kab ka. Tu bhi aa ja.
Anadya(calmly)— Hmm.

Anadya reaches the house.

Anadya— Hello, Aunty. Kaise ho aap?
Aunty(pulling her into a hug)— Anadya! Kitna time ho gaya! Aur itna late — apni hi best friend ki shaadi mein, beta?
Anadya— Sorry, Aunty. Leave hi nahi mil rahi thi.
Aunty— Haan, Jiya ne bataya tha. Par ab toh tu yahan hai — full enjoy kar! Aur jaldi ready ho ja.

Aunty looks around for someone to help with the bags. Anadya spots Mahir passing behind her — head down, busy on his phone, completely unaware.

Anadya— Arey Aunty, main le loongi. Aap guests attend karo — woh wait bhi kar rahe hain aapka.

Episode 02

Jiya's Room

Jiya throws her arms around Anadya the second she walks in.

Jiya(excited)— Anadyaa — meri shaadi ho rahi hai!
Anadya(smiling through it)— Haan… teri shaadi. Wow, congratulations!
Jiya(patting her shoulder, dramatically)— Teri bhi ho jayegi jaldi.
Anadya(joining her hands)— Ohh — abhi tu kar le, pehle.
Jiya— Haan, par tu na — ab kisi ko toh dekh. Mauka de kisi ko, apne aap ko. Warna arrange ho jayegi, yaar.
Anadya(almost disgusted by the idea)— Tu ye sab chhod — aur ye bata, kaisa jaa raha hai sab? All set?
Jiya— Haannn… almost.

A knock at the door.

Jiya— Khol toh — Mahir hoga.

Anadya takes a breath and opens the door — and finds Jiya's friend, Suhani. Relief washes over her face.

Suhani— Hi.
Anadya— Hello.

And then, right behind Suhani, steps in the one person she'd just exhaled about. Mahir enters with a box in hand. Grinning.

Episode 03

Long Time No See?

Suhani(looking Mahir over)— Tu toh ready bhi nahi hua abhi tak?
Mahir(smiling to himself)— Mujhe hone ki zaroorat bhi nahi hai, Suhani.

He steps further in and his eyes find Anadya. She's standing near the door, a polite smile on her face. A small nod. A very formal gesture.

Mahir's eyes go wide. His smile stretches just a little wider than he probably intended.

Mahir(excited)— Oh — Anadya, tu aa gayi. Verryyy long timeee… right?

He starts moving toward her to hug her, then reads her face. He pivots mid-step into an over-enthusiastic handshake instead. His smile softens.

Jiya(already distracted)— Dikha toh — jaldi box de.

He steps forward and his foot catches on a piece of fallen cloth. He stumbles. Anadya reaches out instinctively. Her fingers brush his for just a second.

Mahir(catching himself, zoned out)— Got it. Got it. Thank you, Anadya — all good?

Anadya doesn't say anything. Turns her attention to Jiya.

Jiya— Ek kaam bhi sahi nahi… Chal, Anadya — ye dekh. Ye tere liye hi hai.
Anadya(confused)— Mere liye hai?
Jiya(almost tearing up)— Haan… please, kal subah jaakar isse sahi karwa de. Maine ye order bhi nahi kiya tha, aur kal din ko mujhe pehna hai.
Anadya(holds her by the shoulders)— Haan toh — theek hai. Chill kar. Main chali jaaungi — fix bhi ho jaayega.
Jiya(pulling her into a hug)— Anadya… maine bohot miss kiya hai tujhe. Kaafi emotional ho rahi hoon main.

Anadya holds her tight. Mahir watches them — adoringly.

Anadya— Hota hai. Hota hai — emotional hona bhi chahiye apni shaadi pe, atleast.

She laughs. Jiya laughs too.

Aunty(from outside)— Mahir! Idhar aa!
Mahir(switching to a tired expression)— Haan, aaya.

He heads out — then, two seconds later, barges back in.

Mahir(sarcastic gesturing)— Later guys? Anadya, chal — Mummy bula rahi hai.
Anadya(confused)— Huhh? Haan.

Episode 04

Aunty's Instructions

Aunty— Anadya, beta — please jaldi ready ho ja. Aur iske saath jaakar, kal ki jewellery lekar aa ja. Please.

Mahir and Anadya stand side by side. Anadya shifts from looking lost to assuring.

Anadya— Haan Aunty, isme please ki kya baat hai. Abhi chale jaate hain.

She glances at Mahir.

Anadya— Aap…
Mahir(eyebrow raised)— Aap?
Mahir(sincere yet sarcastic)— Anadya — "aap" kabse bolne lagi tu? Vo bhi mujhe?
Anadya(controlling her expressions)— Aap badde ho.
Mahir(frustrated, disbelieving)— "Aap badde ho"… batao, Mummy? Abhi thodi hua hoon Anadya — bachpan se hoon, aur bas 2 saal bada hoon. Tab toh kabhi izzat nahi ki tune meri.
Aunty(laughing)— Haan Mahir — ek tujhme hi tameez nahi aayi hai bas.
Aunty— Dono ready ho kr jana, function start ho jayega. Aur Anadya — isse "aap" toh mat hi bol, beta. Ye deserving nahi hai.

A guest approaches. Aunty walks away.

Mahir(serious sarcasm)— Haan Anadya aur — "tu" bolna mujhe, please.

He walks away. Sensing something has shifted — and he can feel it.

Episode 05

The Car Ride

Mahir leans against the car, watching Anadya walk toward him. She's dressed in a heavy traditional suit, walking unhurried, avoiding eye contact. He opens the door without a word.

Anadya(a quick glance)— Thank you.

She gets in. Mahir settles into the driver's seat.

Mahir(exhaling)— Aur bata, Anadya — kya chal raha hai?
Anadya(after a pause)— Sab acha jaa raha hai.
Mahir(deadpan)— Sach mein? Thodi detail de-de fir.
Anadya— Job chal rahi hai. Delhi mein. Achi jaa rahi hai.

Mahir nods slowly, a distant look settling on his face as he drives. Anadya notices.

Anadya— Aur… tumhari?
Mahir(glancing at her)— "Teri."

Anadya stares ahead.

Mahir(sarcastic, not letting it go)— Itna formally kyun bol rahi hai? Acha nahi lag raha bilkul. Casual rehte hai na.
Anadya(pauses)— Haan.
Mahir(impatient)— Haan kya — kuch uske aage bol.
Anadya— Teri job kaisi jaa rahi hai?
Mahir(with a straight face)— Bas — achi.

She gives him a look.

Mahir— Dekha — kitna dull sound kiya?
Mahir(playfully)— Ruk, ab ache se bataata hoon. Abhi toh main 16 days ki leave pe hoon. Uske baad soch raha hoon — shift karna chahiye. Jagah change karte rehna chahiye na.
Anadya(listening closely)— Aur kahaan?

Mahir senses a bit of interest finally.

Mahir— Options hain. Ek best lag raha hai.

He looks at her. Anadya tries to read him.

Mahir— Delhi. Tu bhi wahi hai — toh sorted, kyun?
Anadya(a little off)— Haan, sahi hai.

They arrive.

Episode 06

The Jewellery Shop

Mahir takes Aunty's jewellery box to the counter to get it fixed.

Mahir— Ja — tu bhi kuch dekh le. Collection bahut acha hai inka, kyun bhaiya. Main ye karwa raha hoon tab tak.
Anadya— Par nahi, vo —
Mahir— Ja. Jaakar dekh kar toh aa, pehle.

From the counter, Mahir watches Anadya walk toward him holding a set of bangles — beautiful ones. She simply holds them up and looks at him. He smiles anyway and gives a quiet "wow" gesture. She smiles softly — just for a second — and then immediately composes herself.

As she walks toward the counter, Mahir slips back to the shopkeeper.

Mahir(quietly, in a rush)— Bhaiya — ye kitne ka hai? Door se hi dekh ke bata do jaldi. Ye mujhe pay karne nahi degi.

Mahir makes the payment hurriedly.

Mahir— Aur kuch bhi ho jaaye — isko price mat batana, Bhaiya. Please.

He walks back fast toward Anadya, completely casual.

Mahir(rushing)— Anadya, chal — ghar se phone aa raha hai.
Anadya(turning to the shopkeeper)— Payment?
Mahir(deadpan)— Bhaiya ne khud kar di hai.
Anadya— Haan? Tune ki hai na…

Mahir looks away, pretending innocence.

Anadya(seeing through him now)— Tune kiya — yaar Mahir… kitna kiya… chup karke bata de mujhe. Tujhe pata hai mujhe ye sab acha nahi lagta?

She turns to the shopkeeper, trying to get the amount out of him. Mahir doesn't respond — just watches her go frustrated, with a quiet smile. This is the first time today she's been completely herself with him.

Then Jiya calls.

Anadya(on call, staring at Mahir throughout)— Haan — bas jewellery le rahe hain… bas aa rahe hain.

Mahir doesn't break eye contact, a teasing smile on his lips; she finally looks away, awkward and frustrated.

Episode 07

The Car Ride Back

Mahir keeps sneaking glances at Anadya while she stares ahead, until she finally bursts out.

Anadya— Mahir, tujhe batana padega — ye sahi nahi hai. Tu ne kyun diye?
Mahir(being funny)— Kyunki mere paas paise hain — toh tu kyun degi, Anadya?
Anadya— Main serious hoon.

He leans over to play a song.

Mahir— Gana sunegi?
Anadya— Toh fir main bhi krungi ye sab… main bhi dilaungi tujhe. Soch le.
Mahir(smiling, eyes soft)— Tu jo de — main sab accept karoonga. Gifts ke liye main kabhi mana nahi karta, voh bhi tujhse.

Anadya rolls her eyes and turns to the window, smiling. Again, slowly — the smile fades.

Episode 08

The Sangeet

Aunty— Aa gaye dono.

Mahir hands her the bag — then pauses and takes it back.

Mahir— Ek minute, Mummy — Anadya ka samaan bhi hai.
Anadya— Aunty, ye mujhe pay nahi karne de raha.
Aunty(sarcastic)— Beta, kyun karna hai pay — kuch toh usse bhi karne de…

Anadya makes a face like she'd already lost this battle. She shows Aunty the bangles.

Aunty— Bohot sundar hain. Pehen na abhi inhe. Aur haan — ab jaldi jaakar mehendi lagwa le. Maine usse rok ke rakha hai tere liye.
Mahir(casually teasing, almost testing)— Konsa isse kisika naam likhwana hai abhi?

Anadya gives him a side-eye.

Aunty(teasingly)— Tujhe kya pata — koi ho toh… Kyun, Anadya?

The music starts. Anadya stands in a corner, watching Mahir and Jiya dance with the family — genuinely happy, at ease.

Suhani pulls Mahir in. He dances for a bit — then glances around the room until he finds Anadya.

He goes to her and brings her in. She resists. Jiya and Aunty pull her along anyway. They all match steps together.

At some point, Mahir quietly steps back and just enjoys watching her.

Episode 09

Late Night

Everyone in their night clothes. Jiya and Anadya are on the sofa in the hall, talking. Mahir wanders in from outside.

Mahir(suddenly smiles as he spots her)— Anadya — tu kya pi rahi hai?
Anadya(with a straight face)— Coffee.
Mahir(being funny)— Toh tu poori raat jaagegi.
Anadya(annoyed, direct)— Haan.
Mahir(standing right next to her, pouring himself a cup)— Haan toh main bhi pi leta hoon fir. Tujhe company chahiye hogi na, jaagne ke liye.

Suhani stumbles in from the other room — tipsy — taking Mahir's hand.

Suhani— Let's go, Mahir. Let's get you a drink.
Mahir(very quietly, sarcastic)— I don't think you should get any more.

He glances at Anadya, then Jiya, and awkwardly frees his hand.

Mahir(looking at Anadya, flexing)— Actually — I don't drink.
Suhani(flirty)— Seriously? Because your eyes say otherwise.

Jiya smiles. Even Anadya, watching them.

Mahir(big eyes at Anadya and Jiya)— Seriously! No no — ask Jiya. Main nahi karta na. Jiya, bata?

Suhani stumbles. Jiya stands up and takes her to her room.

Mahir stands behind the sofa, casually swings one leg over the back, and slides into the seat right next to Anadya — smooth indeed.

Mahir(hoping to get a reaction)— Yaar Anadya — isko toh main pasand hoon.

Anadya says nothing. Keeps drinking her coffee.

Mahir— Tujhe nahi lagta?
Anadya(dryly)— Haan toh. Achi hai woh, kaafi.
Mahir— Acha toh main hua na fir?

Anadya focused on finishing her coffee.

Mahir(caught off guard by her casualness)— Tujhe kya pata — meri koi ho bhi toh sakti hai.

Anadya looks at him. Takes a sip.

Anadya(very bluntly)— Haan, bata de usse fir. Feelings clear rakh apne end se — kyun kisi ko hurt karna.
Mahir(getting annoyed)— Yaar, meri koi kyun hogi. Aur meri taraf se feelings bilkul clear hain — maine kabhi uska flirt encourage nahi kiya hai.
Anadya— Tu ye sab mujhe kyun bata raha hai?
Mahir— Batana padega na — tu mujhe galat samajh rahi hai. Main humesha apne intentions clear rakhta hoon.
Anadya(standing up)— Tere intentions, tujhe pata.
Mahir(catching her hand)— Meri toh complete hone de.

She stops. Takes a breath. Sits back down. Jiya appears in the doorway.

Jiya— Guys — goodnight. Time se so jaana. Tumhe subah 5 baje nikalna bhi hai.
Anadya(shocked)— Humein?

Mahir looks at Anadya — confused again.

Jiya— Haan — ye hi toh lekar jaayega. Ek ghanta toh jaane mein lagega. Punctual rehna.

Jiya disappears.

Mahir(almost confronting)— Aise react kyun kiya tune — mere saath kyun nahi jaana hai?
Anadya— Khatam ho gayi?
Mahir— Kya?

Anadya glances into his empty cup.

Anadya— Coffee.

She gets up.

Anadya— Goodnight — subah jaana bhi hai.
Mahir(with his sarcastic smile)— Saath chalna hai.

As she walks away, Anadya touches her neck lightly — calming herself. Mahir sinks back into the sofa. Thinking.

Episode 10

3 AM

Anadya comes downstairs quietly. She sees Mahir asleep on the sofa, pauses for a second and walks toward the garden. Mahir suddenly sits up.

Anadya(startled)— Shit — tu soya nahi?
Mahir(fully awake, energetic)— Tu bhi toh nahi soyi.

She keeps walking towards the garden. He follows.

Mahir(sarcastic)— So — coffee, haan?

She sits down in the garden. He sits across from her.

Mahir(out of nowhere, very directly)— Anadya — ek baat puchu? Seriously.
Anadya— Main mazak ke mood mein nahi hoon.
Mahir— Main bhi nahi hoon. Sach mein.

She looks at him. Exhales.

Anadya— Bolo.
Mahir— Kuch hua hai na?
Anadya(sensing his tone)— Kya?

Mahir keeps looking at her — right in her eyes.

Anadya(going crazy)— Mahir, main yahan saas lene aayi hoon —
Mahir(very direct)— Anadya — tu mujhse baat kyun nahi kar rahi hai?

She goes quiet.

Mahir(realizes)— Maine expect kiya tha — par ye nahi kiya tha. Mujhe laga tu gussa karegi. Maine khud ko uske liye prepare kiya tha lekin tu toh chup ho gayi hai.
Anadya(cutting in, angry)— Mahir, tu pagal hai?
Mahir(quietly)— Haan — sach mein hoon. Kaafi time liya hai na maine. Mujhe pata hai main galat hoon. Bohot galat. Ki maine —
Anadya— Mahir — hum ye kyun discuss kar rahe hain abhi?
Mahir(frustrated, emotional)— Kyunki mujhe laga — ki hum dono ye share karte hain. Par tu mujhse baat tak nahi kar rahi. Jaise kuch tha hi nahi kabhi.

Tears fill Anadya's eyes.

Anadya(voice catching, angry)— Ek minute — ye sab abhi kyun aa raha hai? Ye tab kahaan tha jab tu jaa raha tha? Itne saal kahaan tha tu? Kyunki main toh aayi thi na — yaad hoga tujhe. Main toh chahti thi — ki tu kuch bole. Kuch toh bataye. Par tu mere aage se chala gaya. Bas aise hi. Tab kuch nahi tha kya? Bata.
Mahir(barely holding it together)— Kyunki mujhe dar tha, Anadya. Ki main nahi kar paunga — aur main nahi kar paya. Wahaan jaakar main apne ghar tak phone nahi kar paa raha tha. Mujhe pata tha ki — main manage nahi kar paa raha tha. Par jahaan bhi tha, maine tujhe har din socha hai.

He pauses.

Mahir(breaking down)— Tujhe pata hai na — ye sab mujhe karna tha. Is business mein itna invest ho gaya ki baaki sab chhod diya maine. Main kisi se touch mein nahi tha. Aur main aisa nahi tha, Anadya. Main apne logon se door nahi reh paata. Fir bhi — sabse zyada door main tujhse raha. Aur jo ghar aaya tha ye 2 baar — tab bhi nahi rehna chaha. Maine Jiya se tere plans poochhkar apne banaye the — par nahi hua. Aur meri hi galti hai saari.
Mahir— Maine bohot socha — bata doonga, bol doonga. Fir socha — agar bol diya aur kar nahi paya, toh sab khatam ho jaayega, jo kabhi shuru bhi nahi hua tha. Toh aise kuch shuru karne ki himmat hi nahi hui.
Anadya(tears falling, breakdown)— Isliye clarity hi nahi di tune. Mera kya? Aur ab kyun? Kyunki tune tab toh kuch bola hi nahi tha na. Maine connect karne ki koshish ki thi tujhse — par tune calls uthana sahi nahi samjha. Phir main bhi kya hi karti, kya samajhti — main thak gayi thi. Tu ek baar bolta toh — ki tujhe time chahiye tha. Bs itna — par tu toh bas chala gaya. Mujhe sochna tha fir — Aur maine sab kuch socha, jo mujhe nahi sochna chahiye tha. Aur main khatam ho gayi. Kya expect kiya tha tune mujhse, maturity? Aa gayi. Bohot mushkil se aayi — isliye tujhe block kar diya, aur decide bhi kar liya tha ki kuch bhi ho jaaye, main tujhse baat toh nahi karungi. Kabhi nahi karungi. Par tune phir bhi ki. Aur aaj phir wahi ho raha hai jo maine khud ko bohot mushkil se samjhaya tha ki kabhi nahi hoga.
Mahir— Main —
Anadya(breaking)— Aur yahaan aakar mujhe nahi dekhna tha tujhe. Mujhse jitna hua, utna avoid kiya. Lekin tu sab itna easily normal lekar kaise jaa sakta hai?

She breaks down completely. Mahir reaches forward and holds her.

Mahir(softens his voice)— Anadya — main galat hoon. Tu bilkul sahi hai. Galti meri hai ki maine woh kiya jo mere liye easy tha. Please aise mat ro. Tujhe jo bolna hai, bol. Sab bol de.

His eyes get filled with tears.

Anadya(pushing him away)— Tu jaa yahaan se — jaise chala gaya tha.
Mahir(very gently)— Dekh, Anadya — please aise mat kar. Main ab kahin nahi jaaunga. Kabhi bhi. Kasam se. Main ab yahi hoon.
Mahir— Aur mera Delhi waala plan koi mazak nahi tha. Main ye sab tere saath rehne ke liye kar raha hoon. I'm sorry main nahi soch paya ki tere liye ye sab kaisa hoga. Aur aise toh main tujhe kabhi nahi dekhna chahta.
Mahir(barely a whisper)— I'm sorry, Anadya.

Silence.

Mahir— Kuch toh bol — chup mat reh aise.
Anadya(cries)— Tu chala jaa, please.
Mahir(with full sincerity)— Anadya. I'm sorry. Please.

She cries hard. He reaches forward and pulls her close. She pushes him away — then breaks down even harder. He holds her through it, tears in his own eyes.

Mahir(very softly)— Main bohot sorry hoon, Anadya. Kabhi nahi karunga — tujhe bataye bina kuch nahi karunga.

Episode 11

Still In The Garden. Later.

Swollen eyes. Low energy. The night has gone quiet. Anadya sits with her head resting on her knees. Mahir sits beside her, looking at her.

Mahir(very quietly)— Anadya…
Anadya— Hmm.
Mahir— Tune mehendi pe naam likhwaaya tha — phir?
Anadya— Haan — mere boyfriend ka.
Mahir(looking at her)— Oh. Mera naam?
Anadya— Nahi.
Mahir— Achaa.
Anadya— Tune toh itne saalon mein kisi ko date hi nahi kiya hoga jaise?
Mahir— Kiya toh hai.

He looks up at the sky.

Mahir(whispering)— Tujhe apni imagination mein — throughout date kiya hai maine.

Anadya slowly tries to sit straight while looking up at him. He then tries to rest her head on his shoulder. She moves away. So he shifts and rests his own head on her shoulder instead. Then in a few seconds, smoothly, settles into her lap.

Mahir— Ohhh — Anadya. Bohot manifest kiya hai isse maine.

Anadya sits there. Emotional. Still.

Anadya(quietly, slightly taunting)— Ye sb kuch kaise ho raha hai na — khair, tujhe toh pata tha.
Mahir(trying to change it)— 5 bhajne wali hongi. Nikalna padega — for my first long drive with my first girlfriend.
Anadya— Main teri girlfriend nahi hoon.
Mahir(half asleep)— Tu hai. Tujhe bas pata abhi chala hai. Maine itne time jo tujhe mind mein date kiya — uska kya?
Anadya— Mujhe bada bataya tha na tune — tera mind.
Mahir— Yaar, Anadya…

He touches her legs in a smooth way, asking for apology, while still in her lap. Anadya removes his hand gently, smiling — realising his drama.

Anadya(softly)— Chl tu so ja — car bhi chalani hai fir.
Mahir(banter)— Tu bhi so ja — ya sun, abhi mujhe sula de, fir tu car mein so jaana.

He takes her hand and rests it against his cheek. Anadya smiles — and lightly slaps him.

Episode 12

5 AM. The Car.

Anadya is asleep in the passenger seat. Mahir drives, glancing at her every few minutes — smiling each time, quietly enjoying it.

He slows the car. Stops.

Mahir(softly)— GOOD MORNING, ANADYA!
Anadya(still sleepy)— Hmm.
Mahir(excited)— Dekh — kahaan aaye hain hum.

Outside the window — a stunning hilltop viewpoint. The sky is shifting. Perfect sunrise time.

Anadya(realising)— Mahir — kitna beautiful lg rha h yaha se.
Mahir(smiles, deep breath of relief)— Haash — usse zyada beautiful mera naam laga hai mujhe, tere muh se.

She looks at him.

Mahir— Or usse bhi zyada — usse lene wali.

Anadya sensing the flirt, smiling, shy.

Anadya(laughs)— Wow Mahir, nice skills. Wahan develop ki thi?
Mahir(winking, holding eye contact)— It's all you. Naturally aa rha h sb kuch itna, has to be you.
Anadya— Achaa — chalna hai?
Mahir(smiles realising)— Ab tu itna sharma hi rahi hai toh, chal.

They stand outside the car.

Anadya(looking down)— Mahir — main toh chappal mein aayi hoon.

Mahir starts moving toward her.

Mahir— Arey ruk. Main uthata hoon na.

Mahir picks her up. Anadya's eyes go wide. She slaps him lightly on his back.

Anadya(startled, shy)— Nhiii. Neeche rakh. NEECHE RAKH. Main khud chaloongi.
Mahir(shocked, unbelievable)— Yaar — tu toh mauka bhi nahi de rahi. Le, haath toh pakad le chl.
Anadya— Kyun? Tu chal nahi payega?

She starts walking.

Mahir— Anadya — I know tujhe tera boyfriend late mila hai.

He holds her hand. She tries to shake it off — cutely, helplessly — turning red.

Mahir(smiling widely)— Pr ye romantic hota hai. See.

Mahir keeps her hand in his, swinging it back and forth in a teasing way. Anadya shy and imitates his tone right back — pointing to sunrise point.

Anadya— See — sunrise ho jayega.

Episode 13

The Hilltop. Sunrise.

They sit side by side at the top, looking out at the sunrise in silence. Mahir quietly takes one side of his jacket and wraps it around her with his arm.

Anadya gives him a sideways look.

Anadya— Zyada nhi ho raha hai.
Mahir(smiling, drunk on happiness)— Dekh, I am happy. You are warm. "Now" I must keep my girlfriend warm.
Anadya(laughing)— Mujhe literally thand nhi —

He holds her hand and rubs it in his. Anadya smiles and looks at a distance, turns slightly emotional — like something familiar washes over her. Like she gets a past memory of theirs again, remembering their old times.

Anadya(quietly)— Tu ekdum same hai, Mahir.

Mahir senses it. He pulls her closer — tightening his arm around her.

Mahir— Maine bhi tujhe bohot miss kiya, Anadya.

Anadya smiles softly.

Mahir(randomly looking ahead)— Maine — pata hai — aisa kisi ke saath wahaan nahi kiya.
Anadya(slightly annoyed)— Aur tu kyun karta?
Mahir(laughing coz she took it the other way)— Arey — nhi — matlab —

Zooms out. Cut.

Episode 14

Back Home. The Garden. Morning.

They arrive back with the box. Aunty and Jiya are in the garden, mid-conversation. They look up as Mahir and Anadya walk in together.

Jiya(curious)— Aa gaye. Dono?
Aunty(with a knowing smile)— Kaisa raha morning trip, phir?

Anadya straightens up — formal, composed, pretending like nothing's between them.

Mahir(teasing, looking at her)— Bata de, Anadya.

Anadya looks at him, confused, slightly alarmed. Jiya and Aunty exchange a glance. They smile.

Jiya(points and laughs)— Meri hone wali bhabhi.
Mahir(nods happily)— Kaafi help lagi hai inki… tujhe lejaane mein — woh bhi 2 baar. Ek car ride Mummy ne arrange karayi aur ek Jiya ne.

Anadya looks around, stunned — and then gets nervous.

Anadya— Seriously? Sabko pata tha?
Aunty— Nahi — sabko toh abhi btana hai. Iski shaadi ke baad bs.
Mahir(excited, wraps his arm around Anadya)— Next — apni.

Anadya laughs, blushing, as she tries to push his arm away — and everyone laughs with her.
