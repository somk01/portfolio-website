KFC — Independent campaign concept

KFC

Mann Lagega Aur Dhyaan Bhi

An alphabet, a study table, and a sibling who knows exactly what comes after K.

Insight

We're naturally drawn to what others are enjoying. Sometimes, all it takes is one glance to shift everyone's attention.

The film

A mother is sitting with her kid at a study table, teaching.

The kid, fully focused, starts reciting out loud — looking up at the roof.

Kid(pauses at K)— E… F… G… H… I… J… K…

Suddenly the son looks past her —

Kid(with stars in his eyes)— F…

The mother turns back, confused, following his gaze. Spots the older sibling enjoying that crispy crunch — filling the moment.

Older sibling(mid-bite)— C……

Mother gets mesmerised too. Smiles.

Mother— KFC…

Happy smiles and excitement on everyone's faces. Final frame.

Voiceover: "KFC — mann lagega aur dhyaan bhi."

End card

KFC.

Mann lagega aur dhyaan bhi.

Key takeaway

Sometimes the funniest insight is the one any sibling could've told you for free. Good QSR work just bothers to listen.

Independently developed concept · self-initiated exploration · not commissioned by the brand.
