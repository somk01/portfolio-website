Nykaa — Independent campaign concept

Nykaa

We Got You

A bride, a Nani, and a makeup artist who's three steps ahead.

Insight

Weddings have a funny way of testing your preparation. The best way to enjoy the journey is to be prepared for it.

The film

Scene 1 — Car. En route to a destination wedding.

Bride is showing her phone to the Makeup Artist, excited.

Bride— Ye wala soft glam look… lips bold red, okay?
Makeup Artist(calm — adds to cart)— Hmm… hmm.

Bride is now immersed in the Makeup Artist's phone as she adds items to cart.

Scene 2 — Mom observation.

Mom looks at the artist, slightly confused. She turns to the Bride's Nani and whispers.

Mom(glancing at the artist)— Kaafi relaxed nhi si…
Nani(a little loud — then suddenly panicked)— Hmm…
Nani— Oh mera kajal!
Makeup Artist(all chill)— Kardiya.
Nani(confused)— Ki??

Makeup artist calmly shows her the cart.

Scene 3 — Irony.

Mom(now calm, same tone)— Nykaa pe add to cart… tussi tension na lo.

Nani — shocked expression.

End card

Nykaa.

We got you.

Key takeaway

Beauty brands win when they stop selling the look and start selling the feeling of being three steps ahead of your own panic.

Independently developed concept · self-initiated exploration · not commissioned by the brand.
